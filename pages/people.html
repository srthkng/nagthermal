Our Team

The Multiscale Thermal Engineering Laboratory (MTEL) brings together researchers working in heat transfer, fluid mechanics, electronics cooling, battery thermal management, hydrogen energy systems, and advanced thermal technologies. We combine experimental, computational, and theoretical approaches to address multidisciplinary engineering challenges.
