Our Team

The Multiscale Thermal Engineering Laboratory (MTEL) brings together researchers working in heat transfer and fluid mechanics, with an application focus towards electronics cooling, battery thermal management, and advanced thermal technologies. We combine experimental and computational approaches to address multidisciplinary engineering challenges.
