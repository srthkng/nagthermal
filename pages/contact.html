Research Opportunities

The Multiscale Thermal Engineering Laboratory (MTEL) welcomes highly motivated students and researchers interested in:

  • Electronics Cooling
  • Battery Thermal Management
  • Immersion Cooling
  • Microchannel Heat Transfer
  • Hydrogen Energy Systems
  • Nanobubbles and Interfacial Transport
  • Computational and Experimental Heat Transfer
Prospective Students

Applications are welcome from motivated students interested in pursuing PhD, MS (Research), M.Tech, B.Tech projects, internships, or visiting research positions.

Please email your CV, academic transcripts, and a brief statement describing your research interests.
